About Us!

Our mission is to provide a platform for hikers to share their experiences and help others find the best trails. Our trails have been reviewed by our community of hikers and are rated based on difficulty, length, and scenery. We always do our best to provide a well rounded review of each trail, so you can make an informed decision on which trail to hike next. We hope you enjoy our site and find it helpful in planning your next hike!

Ptarmigan Tunnel

Swiftcurrent Pass

Hidden Lake

Hidden Lake trail is a moderate level trail. A trail renowned for its beatiful views and is only 2.8 miles.

Gunsight Lake

A moderate hike only taking 5 and a half hours. With stunning views of turquoise glacial watercolor.

Cracker Lake

A moderate trail with a lot of uphill climbing. With stunning views of Lake Sherburne and apline meadows.
